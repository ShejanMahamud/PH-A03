What is JohuHockey's?

JohuHockey's is an online platform dedicated to our hockey club, providing information, updates, and resources for players, fans, and supporters.

How do I become a member of JohuHockey's?

To become a member, visit the "Membership" page on our website and follow the registration instructions.

Is there an age limit for joining JohuHockey's?

Our hockey club welcomes players of all ages. We have programs for youth, adults, and seniors. Check our age-specific categories during registration.

Do you offer hockey training programs?

Absolutely! Explore our "Training Programs" section to find details about coaching sessions, clinics, and development programs for players of all skill.

How can I contact JohuHockey's for further assistance?

You can reach out to us through the "Contact Us" page. We're here to help with any inquiries, feedback, or support you may need.
